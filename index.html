About Bella Pizza

Our Heritage

Bella Pizza was founded in 1952 by Giovanni Rossi, who immigrated to America from Naples, Italy, bringing with him generations of family pizza-making secrets. What started as a small neighborhood pizzeria has grown into the most beloved pizza destination in the city, but we've never forgotten our humble beginnings.

Giovanni's grandson, Marco Rossi, now leads our kitchen with the same passion and dedication that his grandfather brought to America over 70 years ago. Every pizza we serve honors the traditional Neapolitan methods while embracing the diverse tastes of our community.

Our Philosophy

At Bella Pizza, we believe that great pizza is more than just food ‚Äì it's a way of bringing people together. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, enjoying a casual dinner with family, or grabbing a quick slice on your lunch break, we're honored to be part of your daily life.

We source our ingredients from the finest suppliers, including San Marzano tomatoes directly from Italy, fresh mozzarella made daily, and organic vegetables from local farms. Our wood-fired ovens, imported from Naples, reach temperatures of 900¬∞F to create that perfect crispy-yet-chewy crust that our customers have come to love.

Community Commitment

As a family-owned business, we're deeply committed to giving back to our community. We regularly donate to local schools, sponsor youth sports teams, and participate in charity events throughout the year. When you choose Bella Pizza, you're not just getting great food ‚Äì you're supporting a business that cares about making our neighborhood a better place.
